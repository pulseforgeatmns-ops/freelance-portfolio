Common questions

Things people ask.

What if I'm not sure which tier fits?

Book the strategy call. Fifteen minutes, free, no pitch deck. I'll look at your business, your offer, and your goals, and recommend the right tier. Sometimes I'll recommend starting at Starter and moving up. Sometimes I'll tell you Pulseforge isn't the right fit at all. Either is fair.

Are there setup fees or annual commitments?

No on both. Onboarding is included in your first month. Billing is monthly, and you can cancel with seven days' notice before the next billing cycle. I've structured it this way because the system either earns its keep or you walk.

Do you offer free trials?

Not as a published policy, but yes in specific cases. If you're an early-adopter operator in a vertical or market I'm trying to learn, mention it on the call. The current founding-client cohort all started on trial structures.

What if I'm an agency looking to white-label?

Talk to me about Enterprise. I've built Pulseforge to operate cleanly across multiple brand identities, and the white-label option exists for that reason. Margins and terms are quoted per engagement.

What guarantees can you make about results?

None. No agency that's being honest can guarantee specific results. Too much depends on your offer, your market, and the quality of your follow-up. What I can promise is the work itself: agents running, sends going out, replies being routed, content being published, and a daily digest showing all of it. The work happens whether or not the leads close.

Where does my data go?

Into Pulseforge's hosted database, with strict separation per client. It's used exclusively to deliver your services and never sold, never used to train anything, never combined with other clients' data. The full handling rules are in the Privacy Policy.
