The problem

Your loyalty list
is sleeping.

Most independent restaurants invest in a loyalty program, collect emails at the host stand or through their POS, and then do absolutely nothing with them.

Not because they don't want to. Because doing it right takes time, tools, and copywriting skills that most owners don't have — and can't afford to hire for.

So the list grows. The emails sit there. And every new member who signs up and never hears from you is a return visit that didn't happen.

Pulseforge fixes this. We build the automation once, write the emails in your voice, and connect it to your loyalty program. Then it runs on its own — forever.

Case study

Built for
OTTO Bedford.

Live — Bedford, NH

The First 30 Days

OTTO Bedford — Pizza / Italian-American

OTTO Bedford is a scratch-made pizza spot in the Market & Main neighborhood hub. They had a Toast loyalty program collecting member emails — and no system to follow up with them.

We built a 5-email welcome sequence that fires the moment someone joins, delivers a personal welcome from owner Shawn, highlights signature pizzas like the Pasta alla Vodka and the Chopped Italian, and brings members back with a 10% welcome offer over 26 days.

Every member gets a unique promo code. Every send is logged. The whole thing runs without a single hour of staff time.
